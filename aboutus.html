About Us

Welcome to our African restaurant located in the heart of Birmingham. We take pride in offering an unforgettable culinary experience that celebrates the rich flavors and vibrant culture of Africa.

With our dedication to excellence, we have been awarded an esteemed three Michelin stars, a testament to our commitment to delivering exceptional dining experiences. Our passionate team of chefs, hailing from Nigeria, brings their expertise and creativity to every dish, ensuring an authentic and tantalizing journey through African cuisine.

At our restaurant, we believe in using only the freshest ingredients sourced locally and from the diverse regions of Africa. Each plate is carefully crafted to capture the essence of African flavors, combining traditional recipes with innovative techniques.

From aromatic tagines to mouthwatering jollof rice, our menu showcases a range of exquisite dishes that cater to all tastes. Whether you are a fan of spicy West African cuisine or prefer the subtle flavors of East African delicacies, our extensive menu is designed to satisfy every palate.

Step into our vibrant and welcoming ambiance, where you'll be transported to the heart of Africa through our warm hospitality and stylish decor. Our attentive staff is dedicated to providing impeccable service, ensuring your dining experience is nothing short of extraordinary.

Join us at our African restaurant in Birmingham and embark on a culinary journey like no other. We invite you to indulge in the flavors, embrace the culture, and create lasting memories with us.
